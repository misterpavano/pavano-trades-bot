## Key Learnings

1. Broken news headlines = invalid news score. Validate that top_headline is non-empty and non-template before awarding points. A news score on garbage text is pure noise.

2. Dollar premium beats contract count. TSLA call score was 7.8 (contract-weighted) but put premium dominated in dollars. Smart money speaks in dollars. Weight put_premium_est vs call_premium_est more heavily than sweep counts.

3. 8 DTE with 5.9% OTM is a lottery ticket. Tighten DTE minimum to 12-14 days. Tighten OTM filter to 3-4% max at entry for directional plays.

4. Zero wins in 6 trades is the signal. Before scaling, diagnose why every trade has closed red. Pattern: EOD closes and stop hits — not targets. Thesis isn't playing out in time. Either the signal scoring is too loose, entries are too far OTM, or DTE is too short.

5. BMNR is the better-structured trade today: real catalyst, call premium dominance, 16 DTE. That is what a good setup looks like. TSLA was a compromise trade.
