## Key Learnings

1. Investigate BAC direction bug immediately. direction=SHORT but CALL entered. Either signal or execution layer has a bug mapping direction to PUT/CALL.
2. EOD flow signals are stale by open. Signals scanned at 15:30 prior day. By 9:30 next morning the gap often invalidates the thesis. Validate confirmation gap at open before entering.
3. Bearish macro regime. LONG signals struggling in risk-off tape. Consider filtering out LONGs when SPY/QQQ in confirmed downtrend.
4. Low-premium contracts inflate qty. Need a max-contracts cap (e.g., 10 max regardless of premium price).
5. PLTR was the best signal today and went untraded. Understand the filter that blocked it.
