## Key Learnings

1. **Fix entry price logging first.** If entry is 0, P&L calculation is garbage. This is a data integrity problem that makes the performance record unreliable.

2. **Add OTM% cap to strike selection.** Any strike more than 15-20% OTM from spot should be rejected. The TSLA $105 signal should never reach execution.

3. **Signal score is not a predictor of outcomes.** High scores lost; low score is winning. Need to rethink whether score threshold should move to 7+ and pair with sweep ratio confirmation.

4. **EOD forced close was the kill shot.** Almost every loss happened because positions closed before the thesis had time to work. The new hold logic is the right fix — let it run.

5. **NVDA and PLTR are the first real test of hold logic.** If they produce a win over the next few days, that validates the shift away from same-day close.
